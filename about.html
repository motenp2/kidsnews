Who We Are

About The Kid Reporter

A motenbased super amazing news paper digital newspaper written, edited, and published entirely by young journalists aged 8–14. Every story is real. Every reporter is a kid.

Our Mission

The Kid Reporter was founded on one simple idea: kids have important things to say, and the world should hear them. We give young people a real platform — not a school project, but a genuine digital publication — to report on the stories that matter to them and their communities.

Our reporters cover real news: science experiments, local government, sports, arts, and the big issues that affect kids everywhere. They interview real people, fact-check their work, and have their articles reviewed by our adult advisory board before publication.
