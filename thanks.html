Our Thank You Wall

Every name on this page is a reason The Kid Reporter exists. Subscribers and donors are not just supporters — they are the foundation of independent youth journalism in Sacramento.

“When a grown-up believes in what a kid is doing, it changes everything. We write better, we try harder, and we feel like our voices actually matter. Thank you for believing in us.”
— Sarai & Alani Moten, Reporters

Every article we publish, every crossword puzzle, every horoscope — it all exists because real people decided that kid-written journalism is worth supporting. You did that. You are listed below not as a transaction, but as a partner in everything we build.

If you would like your name added to this wall, subscribe for $1/month or make a donation. We update this page every week.

With enormous gratitude,
Sarai Moten & Alani Moten
